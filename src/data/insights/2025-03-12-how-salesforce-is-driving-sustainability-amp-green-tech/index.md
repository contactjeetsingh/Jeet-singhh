## Salesforce’s Commitment to Sustainability

### **1\. Net Zero Cloud: Helping Businesses Track & Reduce Carbon Emissions**

Salesforce’s **Net Zero Cloud** is a groundbreaking platform that helps businesses measure and reduce their carbon footprint. Traditionally, organizations struggled to track **emissions data** across different operations, supply chains, and geographies. Net Zero Cloud simplifies this process by providing **real-time carbon accounting**, allowing businesses to:

- **Track Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions** (direct emissions, energy consumption, and supply chain emissions).
- **Set and monitor carbon reduction goals** using AI-driven insights.
- **Generate sustainability reports** to comply with environmental regulations like the **Paris Agreement, SEC Climate Disclosure Rules, and Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD)**.

By leveraging **real-time analytics and AI-driven forecasting**, companies using **Net Zero Cloud** can make informed decisions to reduce their environmental impact.

### **2\. 100% Renewable Energy-Powered Operations**

Salesforce has committed to powering **100% of its operations with renewable energy**. This includes running **energy-efficient data centers** that operate on wind, solar, and hydroelectric power. By transitioning away from fossil fuels, Salesforce significantly reduces its **corporate carbon footprint** and encourages other companies to follow suit.

Additionally, Salesforce is investing in **energy-efficient cloud computing**, ensuring that businesses using Salesforce solutions have a **lower environmental impact compared to traditional on-premise systems**.

### **3\. Sustainability in Supply Chain Management**

Salesforce understands that achieving **true sustainability** extends beyond its own operations. The company actively collaborates with **suppliers, vendors, and partners** to adopt greener practices, including:

- Encouraging **low-carbon manufacturing** for hardware and IT equipment.
- Reducing waste through **recycling and responsible e-waste management**.
- Implementing **sustainable logistics solutions** to minimize transportation emissions.

By integrating **environmental responsibility** into supply chain management, Salesforce ensures that sustainability efforts are embedded at every level of business operations.

## Green Technology & Innovations in Salesforce

### **1\. AI-Powered Sustainability Insights**

Salesforce leverages **Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML)** to provide companies with **sustainability insights and recommendations**. Einstein AI, the AI-driven analytics tool within Salesforce, helps businesses:

- **Optimize energy consumption** by predicting usage patterns.
- **Identify sustainability risks** and suggest mitigation strategies.
- **Automate carbon reporting** for compliance with ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) standards.

These AI-powered capabilities allow businesses to make data-driven decisions that align with their **climate action goals**.

### **2\. Cloud Computing vs. Traditional IT: The Greener Alternative**

Traditional on-premise IT infrastructure consumes large amounts of energy for **server maintenance, cooling systems, and physical storage**. Salesforce’s **cloud computing model** eliminates these inefficiencies by offering:

- **Shared resources and optimized server usage**, reducing overall energy consumption.
- **Scalable solutions that prevent unnecessary hardware expansion**.
- **A fully virtualized infrastructure** that cuts down on **physical waste** and **data center emissions**.

By migrating to cloud-based solutions like Salesforce, businesses can significantly **lower their IT carbon footprint**.

### **3\. Blockchain for Transparent Sustainability Reporting**

Salesforce is exploring **blockchain technology** to enhance **transparency in sustainability reporting**. By using blockchain, organizations can:

- Maintain **tamper-proof environmental data**.
- Ensure **verifiable carbon offset claims**.
- Improve supply chain **traceability for ethically sourced materials**.

This level of transparency helps businesses and consumers trust that sustainability claims are backed by **accurate and immutable data**.

## Salesforce’s Role in Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

### **1\. Philanthropy & Community Engagement**

Salesforce is dedicated to **corporate social responsibility (CSR)** and actively invests in **environmental sustainability programs**. Through its **1-1-1 model**, Salesforce donates **1% of equity, 1% of product, and 1% of employee time** to charitable causes, including:

- **Environmental conservation projects** like reforestation and ocean cleanup efforts.
- **Funding climate action startups** that develop green technologies.
- **Partnering with NGOs** to promote sustainability awareness.

This approach ensures that **environmental impact reduction is not just a corporate initiative, but a community-driven movement**.

### **2\. Encouraging Sustainable Business Practices for Clients**

Salesforce encourages its customers to **adopt sustainable business practices** by providing:

- **Customizable dashboards** for tracking corporate sustainability goals.
- **Automated compliance tracking** to ensure businesses follow environmental regulations.
- **Sustainability-focused integrations** with third-party green tech applications.

This support empowers businesses of all sizes to **incorporate sustainability into their operational strategies**.
