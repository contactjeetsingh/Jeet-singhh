## 1\. New Features That May Appear in Exams

Salesforce frequently updates certification exams to reflect the most recent platform capabilities. While the exact timing may vary, these updates typically occur shortly after major seasonal releases like Spring, Summer, and Winter.

Key platform changes in **Spring '25** that may be reflected in exams include:

- **Enhanced Dynamic Forms Support**  
    Dynamic Forms are now available for more standard objects, which could be included in Admin, App Builder, and Experience Cloud Consultant exams.
    
- **Conditional Formatting for Reports**  
    This feature continues to evolve and may be tested under analytics and reporting sections.
    
- **Einstein Search and AI Enhancements**  
    With the growing integration of AI tools in Salesforce, you may see new questions focusing on these capabilities across multiple certifications.
    

## 2\. Maintenance Modules Are Mandatory

If you're already certified, don't forget to complete your maintenance requirements. These short learning modules ensure that your knowledge is aligned with the current Salesforce release cycle. For Spring '25, maintenance updates focus on:

**Failing to complete maintenance on time can result in your certification being expired**, so stay alert to Trailhead notifications or check your credential status regularly.
