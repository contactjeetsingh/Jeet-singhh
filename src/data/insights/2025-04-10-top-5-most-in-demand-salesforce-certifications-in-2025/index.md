## What Are Salesforce Certifications?

Salesforce certifications are credentials that validate your skills on the Salesforce platform. Each certification is tailored to a specific role — such as Administrator, Developer, Consultant, or Architect — and confirms your ability to use Salesforce to solve real-world business problems. Getting certified shows you’re job-ready, knowledgeable, and serious about your career in tech.
